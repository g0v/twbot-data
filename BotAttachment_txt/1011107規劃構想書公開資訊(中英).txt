臺南市政府海安路地下停車場ROT或OT案
（規劃構想書）公開資訊        
規劃構想書公開期間：101年11月8日至101年11月22日
初步規劃或營運構想：
案名
簡要內容
申請人一
停車場經營，以經營維護管理計畫、配合內部整體及環境規畫，提升停車場服務水準；逐步整修商場範圍為文化創意展場及里民活動空間。
申請人二
停車場經營，結合地方人文藝術、綠色環保、重視社會公益超值服務的停車場；以創新商業模式逐期投資商場區。
路面停車格位初步規畫構想（需政府協助事項）。
申請人三
停車場經營，停車場安全維護；保留商場部分協助辦理變更使用執照。
申請人四
停車場經營，五大類別整體提昇計畫；初期以委託停車場經營為主，俟時機邀請合適業態廠商參與商場經營。

使用政府土地設施：中西區海安路地下停車場（地下一、二層）






The Public Information of Tainan City Government Hai’an Rd. Underground Parking Garage Project
 (Planning for ROT/OT)

Announcement Period: Nov. 8th, 2012 – Nov. 22rd, 2012
Preliminary Planning and Operation：

Project 
Brief Information
Applicant Ⅰ
The applicant is expected to operate parking garage with maintenance and management plans, to combine with interior and exterior circumstantial planning to enhance service quality, and to renovate shopping district for being the cultural plaza and residents’ activity area.
Applicant Ⅱ
The applicant is expected to operate parking area with the combination of local humanity and arts, environmental protection and social welfare, and to invest shopping district by means of innovative business models.
Preliminary Planning for surface car parking space is required. (Official assistance can be listed).
Applicant Ⅲ
The applicant is expected to operate car parks with safety maintenance, and to preserve part of shopping district for the change of use license.
Applicant Ⅳ
The applicant is expected to operate parking garage with the five- major- category improvement plans.  In the early stage, the applicant is expected to operate parking garage only, and to invite qualified firms for business cooperation by the right time.

Use of Governmental Land and Facilities:
Hai’an Rd. Underground Parking Garage (Basement 1&2).
